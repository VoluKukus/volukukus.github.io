Ojol Simulator

Ojol Simulator is a fun and exciting simulation game where you play as an online motorcycle taxi (ojek online) driver. Your mission is to deliver food and transport passengers around the city as quickly and safely as possible. Earn money from each successful delivery and use it to buy new, faster, and cooler motorcycles. You can also customize and upgrade your bikes to make them truly your own.

Experience the daily life of an ojol driver, take on different challenges, and climb your way to become the top-rated driver in the city!
